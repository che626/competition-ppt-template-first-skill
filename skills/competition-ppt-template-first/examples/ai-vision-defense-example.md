# Example: AI Vision Report to Template Map

This is a generic example of how a report about fabric inspection, model deployment, and a working system should be split before templates are generated. Replace all facts, metrics, assets, and labels with verified project material.

## Content analysis summary

- **Deck type**: competition defense with a working-product proof burden.
- **Audience decision**: the project is not only a detector; it turns visual recognition into an operable, reviewable workflow.
- **Visual ingredients**: fabric macro texture, defect sample, camera / inspection process, real detection image, system screenshot, ONNX / deployment artifact, test record, award or paper.
- **Contrast plan**: rich industrial cover and conclusion; mid-tone material or process pages for problem and solution; light or neutral evidence pages for comparison, system screenshots, and documents. Do not make every page navy and cyan.

| Slide | Story role and takeaway | Background / visual ingredients | Real asset slot and text plan | Contrast mode |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 01 Cover | The project turns visual recognition into a usable inspection workflow. | Inspection process with fabric material and camera optics; project identity is the focal point. | Real product or process photo when available; large title in intentional low-detail space. | Rich, subject-led dark or mid |
| 02 Industry problem | Manual inspection struggles with consistency, records, and follow-up. | Real inspection scene plus fabric macro / sample detail; three failure zones integrated into the scene. | Defect sample or process image; three short evidence blocks and a bottom conclusion. | Mid or light, depending on source image |
| 03 Design scheme | Visual recognition becomes structured, traceable inspection information. | Center transformation: real fabric sample or detection image -> structured result; camera / material detail supports the process. | Left input image, central transformation, right outcome / record, one-line loop at bottom. | Mid |
| 04 Model choice | The selected model best fits the verified deployment goal. | Quiet technical surface with a small material or detection-result cue; no fake dashboard. | Editable comparison matrix and one real detection/deployment artifact. | Light or neutral high-contrast |
| 05 Model iteration | Improvement comes from dataset, validation, and deployment iteration. | Three visibly different artifacts: early sample, revised sample/metric, deployed result; continuous material path. | Each stage gets a real artifact, short change note, and a bottom conclusion. | Light / mid |
| 06 System iteration | The product expanded from inference to an operable system. | Real interface progression or deployment stack with restrained material context. | Large version screenshots with short labels showing what changed. | Neutral / quiet |
| 07 System showcase: primary workflow | The main inspection interface supports the user’s core task. | Screenshot-led composition; small camera/material cue only as supporting context. | Large real product screenshots, callouts placed beside the features that matter. | Neutral / quiet |
| 08 System showcase: configuration | Configuration and records make the system maintainable. | Screenshot-led composition with a different arrangement from Slide 07. | Real settings, records, or management screenshots with concise annotations. | Light or neutral |
| 09 Results and evidence | Papers, records, awards, or tests support credibility. | Evidence wall with document texture or a light gallery surface. | Large readable real certificates / papers; short explanation column. | Light |
| 10 Future roadmap | The next steps are concrete scenario expansion, not generic ambition. | Product or inspection scenario expands into future operating contexts. | Completed / in-progress / future milestones, each tied to a scene or operational asset. | Mid |
| 11 Conclusion | The project’s value is a closed loop from seeing to acting. | Reworked hero process scene with material and camera; no repeated cover clone. | Minimal closing copy and optional real product image. | Rich, subject-led dark or mid |
